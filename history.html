History of The Landmarks

Marina Bay Sands (MBS) opened on April 27, 2010, with a phased opening and officially opened on June 23, 2010. Construction began in 2006, and was originally planned to open in 2009. The building contains 3 57-storey towers, making the complex 57 stories tall or 198 metres tall. Surprisingly, it is the 34th largest building in the world! The top of the 3 towers is a boat shaped building where there is a large swimming pool, called the Infinity Pool.
The National Day Parade (NDP) is an annual parade held in Singapore to commemorate its independence. Held annually on 9 August, it is the main public celebration of National Day, and consists of a parade incorporating contingents of the Singapore Armed Forces, Ministry of Home Affairs, local institutions and community groups, followed by a cultural presentation featuring music, dance displays, parachuters, and a fireworks show.
